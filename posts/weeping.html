--- layout: post title: My weeping heart date: March 4, 2024 ---

I sit with a gaping hole in my heart

But I know it is not empty

It is filled with his love and his spirit

That live on in me.

 

He is gone from this world

But he is not gone from my life

He is in every memory and every moment

That I cherish and hold tight.

All meaning is lost

Gone with him

Into the roiling waves of the Bay of Bengal

Where we tossed his ashes and bones

I need to find my meaning once more, recreate it

Recreate it because that’s what he would want me to do… I know it is not lost

It is in his legacy and his values

That he taught me all his life.

 

My handsome daddy

Who had a love for me

As pure as the morning snow on Mount Fuji

As luminous as the light of a blue star

As comforting as a woolen blanket

on a cold winter afternoon

As ever-present as my own breath.

 

My daddy, whose heart was full of kindness

Whose head was full of ideas

My daddy, whose passing hurts me

Like nothing has ever hurt me

My daddy who was the greatest gift

Of my early life

What a lucky girl I am

To be loved so unconditionally by him

To be loved so by a man like him.

 

I sit in a café, buttering my croissant

Stirring my warm coffee

And there they come…

The menories, the pain

The feeling of being bereft

Like nothing matters now

Not accolades, not achievements, not riches

Why would they, when I can share them

With him no more.

 

I ask myself a million times

How could he die so soon?

How could he not be in this world anymore?

How could I live without hearing his laughter

Without saying a million inanities to each other

How? How? How?

I can find no answer in my weeping heart.
